frequently asked questions

What types of contaminants do you specialize in removing?

We specialize in removing a wide range of contaminants, including but not limited to heavy metals, hydrocarbons, pesticides, solvents, and other hazardous chemicals. Our tailored remediation strategies address specific contaminants based on site assessments.

How do you assess the extent of contamination on a site?h6>

We conduct detailed site assessments, which may include soil and groundwater sampling, geophysical surveys, and data analysis. These assessments help us understand the extent and nature of contamination, allowing us to design effective remediation plans.

Do you offer post-remediation monitoring services?

Yes, we establish post-remediation monitoring programs to ensure the effectiveness and sustainability of our solutions. Regular monitoring includes soil and groundwater testing, vegetation health assessments, and ecosystem evaluations to confirm the site's recovery.

frequently asked questions

Does sustainable construction cost more?

While initial costs for sustainable construction can be slightly higher, the long-term benefits often outweigh the initial investment. Energy savings, reduced operational costs, and potential tax incentives can make sustainable construction financially viable. Additionally, the societal and environmental benefits far exceed any upfront costs. In otherwords, you will spend slighty higher for a sustainable construction project but will save much more in the long run.

Can existing buildings be made sustainable?

Yes, existing buildings can be retrofitted to become more sustainable. Through energy-efficient upgrades, better insulation, eco-friendly materials, and smart technologies, existing structures can be adapted to reduce their environmental impact and improve energy efficiency.

How does sustainable construction benefit communities?

Sustainable construction enhances communities by improving air and water quality, creating green spaces, conserving resources, and promoting healthier living conditions. It can also stimulate local economies by generating green jobs and encouraging sustainable practices within the community.

Our Campaign

Chicago Tent Drice

Chicago Tent Drive" is a compassionate campaign aimed at gathering tents and essential winter supplies for homeless individuals in Chicago. Through community donations and outreach efforts, this initiative strives to provide warmth, shelter, and basic necessities to those in need during the colder months, ensuring a safer and more comfortable environment for the homeless population in the city. Join us in making a difference and spreading warmth to those experiencing homelessness in Chicago.
